Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 29, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Reetoul Image Wizard. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your information when you use our online image processing tools.

2. Information We Collect

At Reetoul Image Wizard, we prioritize your privacy. Our platform is designed to process images entirely client-side, which means:

  • We do not collect, store, or upload your images to any server
  • All image processing happens directly in your web browser
  • Your images never leave your device

The only data we may collect is anonymous, non-personal usage statistics to improve our service.

3. Client-Side Processing

Our commitment to privacy is reflected in our technical architecture. All image processing tools, including renaming, format conversion, and other manipulations, are performed using client-side JavaScript. This means:

  • Image modifications happen in your browser's memory
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Your images remain completely private and under your control

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal, privacy-focused cookies to:

  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze site performance
  • Provide essential website functionality

Users can disable cookies in their browser settings if desired. Doing so may limit some website features.

5. Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party services that prioritize user privacy:

  • CDN providers for fast content delivery
  • Analytics services with anonymized data collection
  • Performance and security monitoring tools

These services adhere to strict data protection guidelines and do not collect personally identifiable information.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all website communications
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Protection against potential web vulnerabilities

7. User Rights

As a user of Reetoul Image Wizard, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to request deletion of any stored information
  • Right to opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Right to know what data is collected about you

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users are encouraged to review the policy regularly. Continued use of the website after changes constitute acceptance of the new terms.

9. Contact Information

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at: privacy@reetoul.com
