Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats does reetoul Image Wizard support?

reetoul Image Wizard supports a variety of image formats including PNG, JPG, and WebP. It can also convert images to PDF format.

Is there a file size limit for uploads?

While there's no strict file size limit, we recommend keeping uploads under 20MB for optimal performance. Larger files may take longer to process.

Can I use reetoul Image Wizard on my mobile device?

Yes! reetoul Image Wizard is fully responsive and works on all devices, including smartphones and tablets. You can easily rename and convert images on the go.

Is my data safe when using reetoul Image Wizard?

We take data privacy seriously. All image processing is done client-side, meaning your images never leave your device. We don't store any of your images or personal information on our servers.

Can I batch process multiple images at once?

Currently, reetoul Image Wizard processes one image at a time. However, we're working on adding batch processing capabilities in a future update. Stay tuned!
